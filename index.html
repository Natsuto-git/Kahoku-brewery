Itinerary

A relaxed 3-hour experience filled with food, culture, and conversation.

  1. 18:00
    Welcome & Kanpai (Ice-breaking)

    Kick off the night with a 'Kanpai'! Grab a drink, introduce yourself, and break the ice. Let's turn strangers into friends before we start cooking!

  2. 18:30
    Takoyaki Party & Dinner

    The main event! 90 minutes of Takoyaki madness. Learn the secret techniques from locals, flip your own octopus balls, and enjoy them piping hot. We have plenty of fillings to try!
