About This Experience

A Sake Experience for Curious Minds

This is not a typical sake tasting. Hosted by Kahoku Brewery (河北酒造), a historic sake brewery from Daigo Town, this pairing experience explores terroir through 4 premium sake selections paired with local delicacies and the art of koji brewing.

Designed for curious travelers, food lovers, and cultural explorers, this experience explores how sake is enjoyed in Japan—shaped by geography, climate, and everyday life.

伝統を受け継ぎながらも、自由な発想で新しい挑戦を続けています。大子町の豊かな自然に囲まれ、寒暖差のある気候の中で丁寧に日本酒を醸しています。
